Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Introduction

VinylCollect ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the VinylCollect mobile application (the "App").

Information We Collect

Photos you take or select: Images are processed by our AI service (Google Gemini) to identify vinyl records. Images are sent to Google's servers for analysis and are not stored by us after processing.

Collection data: Record names, values, and metadata you save are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data is not transmitted to our servers.

Purchase information: If you subscribe, payment is processed by Apple (App Store) through RevenueCat. We do not have access to your payment details.

Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage data (e.g., feature usage, crash reports) to improve the App. No personally identifiable information is included.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide vinyl record identification and valuation services
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To improve the App and user experience

Third-Party Services

  • Google Gemini API: Processes images for AI identification. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Discogs API: Used to enrich record metadata. Subject to Discogs Privacy Policy.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status. Subject to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple App Store: Processes payments. Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Your collection data is stored locally on your device. You can delete all data at any time from the App's Collection screen. Uninstalling the App removes all local data.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at: support@vinylcollect.app
